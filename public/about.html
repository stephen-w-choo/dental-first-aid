About this site
All recommendations are based off the current dental trauma guidelines (linked above), as well as the author's own experience in dental practice.

The author is a dentist located in Melbourne, who was inspired to make this website after encountering two children whose well-meaning parents had re-implanted their baby teeth after they had been knocked out. Which you should never do for baby teeth, by the way.
