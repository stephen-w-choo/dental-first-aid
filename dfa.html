A note before continuing

This guide is specifically to recommend actions after damage to teeth caused by physical injury, such as a fall or other accident.

Following this guide will improve the chances that your dentist will be able to save the tooth. It may also reduce the cost and complexity of treatment required.

These actions are not a substitute for seeing a dentist, and with the exception of extremely minor injuries, you MUST see a dentist for definitive, long-term treatment of the tooth.
