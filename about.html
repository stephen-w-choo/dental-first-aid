About this site
The author is a practicing dentist located in Melbourne, Victoria. He was inspired to make this website after encountering two children whose well-meaning parents had re-implanted their baby teeth after they had been knocked out. Which you should never do for baby teeth, by the way.

All recommendations are based off the current international dental trauma guidelines (linked above), as well as the author's own experience in dental practice.
